## üí° Pro Tips

### Posting Strategy
- Post Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11am or 7-9pm
- Don't spam all groups at once (spread over 3-4 days)
- Respond to EVERY comment within 24 hours
- Be helpful even to people who don't sign up

### Messaging Strategy
- Lead with pain point, not features
- "Stop spending hours on manual repricing" > "4 pricing strategies"
- Show, don't tell (screenshot of repricing history)
- Be humble: "Would love feedback on if this is useful"

### Follow-Up Strategy
- Day 3: "Any issues getting set up?"
- Day 7: "Has it worked for you?"
- Day 14: "What do you think so far?"
- Day 21: "Would you pay for this?"

### Red Flags to Watch
- No one responds to outreach ‚Üí Wrong messaging/audience
- Sign up but never use ‚Üí Bad onboarding
- Use it once, never again ‚Üí Not valuable enough
- Love it but won't pay ‚Üí Pricing mismatch
