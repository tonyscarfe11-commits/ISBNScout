Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 24, 2025

ISBNScout ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our book scouting application and services.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (optional)
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment processors)

Scanning Data

When you use ISBNScout to scan books, we collect:

  • ISBN numbers and book metadata
  • Images you capture (barcodes, covers, spines)
  • Your notes and annotations on scans
  • Pricing and profit data associated with scans
  • Scan timestamps and locations (if you enable location services)

Usage Information

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (type, operating system, browser)
  • IP address and general location
  • App usage statistics and feature interactions
  • Error logs and performance data

Third-Party Platform Data

If you connect Amazon or eBay accounts:

  • API tokens and authentication credentials
  • Seller account information
  • Listing data and inventory information
  • Sales and repricing history

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve ISBNScout services
  • Process your scans and provide pricing analysis
  • Sync data across your devices
  • Create and manage listings on Amazon and eBay
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send service notifications and updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

ISBNScout stores data locally on your device to enable offline functionality. This includes scan history, cached pricing data, and app preferences. Local data is encrypted and syncs to our secure cloud servers when you're online.

Cloud Storage

Your account data and scan history are stored securely on PostgreSQL databases hosted on trusted cloud infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Encryption at rest
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular backups

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

Service Providers

  • Payment processors (Stripe) for billing
  • Cloud hosting providers for data storage
  • Analytics services for app improvement
  • Email service providers for notifications

Third-Party Platforms

  • Amazon and eBay APIs (only when you connect accounts and authorize access)
  • Google Books API for book metadata

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your scan history
  • Opt out of marketing emails
  • Disconnect third-party platform integrations

Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we're required to retain it for legal purposes.

6. Cookies and Tracking

ISBNScout uses essential cookies for authentication and app functionality. We also use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with our app. You can control cookie preferences in your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

ISBNScout is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16. If you believe we've collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. International Users

ISBNScout is based in the United Kingdom and primarily serves UK users. Your data may be processed in the UK or other countries where our service providers operate. By using ISBNScout, you consent to this transfer.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or app notification. Continued use of ISBNScout after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@isbnscout.com
  • Support: support@isbnscout.com

11. UK GDPR Compliance

ISBNScout complies with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR). You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data (Subject Access Request)
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (Right to be Forgotten)
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@isbnscout.com.
