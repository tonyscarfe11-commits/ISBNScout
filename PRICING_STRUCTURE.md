## Pricing Psychology

### Why It Works

1. **Charm Pricing (Â£X.99)**
   - Perceived as cheaper than round numbers
   - Industry standard

2. **Good-Better-Best**
   - Three tiers creates choice
   - Middle option (Pro) sells best
   - Enterprise anchors Pro as "good value"

3. **Most Popular Badge**
   - Social proof
   - Directs attention to Pro
   - Increases conversion

4. **Feature Gating**
   - Shelf scanning only on Pro+
   - Creates desire for upgrade
   - Justifies higher price

5. **Per-Scan Value**
   - Higher tiers = better value
   - Encourages upsell
   - Rational justification
