Frequently asked questions.

Does ISBNScout work without WiFi or mobile data?
Yes. It's offline-first. Scans, AI recognition, and local profit calculations continue to work when you're offline. When your connection returns, data syncs to the cloud.
Is this built for Amazon UK sellers?
Yes. ISBNScout integrates with the official Amazon Seller Partner API and is focused on UK-based sellers using FBA and MFN.
Does it support eBay UK?
Yes. You can see eBay sold data and create listings directly from within ISBNScout using your eBay UK account.
Is the AI spine recognition real?
Yes. You can photograph a single spine or a whole shelf. The AI reads titles and helps you identify which books are worth pulling out and scanning further.
Can I export my inventory or scan history?
Your data is stored in the cloud and export tools are on the roadmap. Early users will help shape which export formats are supported first.
Who is ISBNScout for?
It's designed for part-time and full-time UK book resellers sourcing from charity shops, car-boot sales, and other physical locations.
