## Tips for Outreach Success

### DO:
‚úÖ Be genuine and helpful
‚úÖ Respond to every comment/DM within 24 hours
‚úÖ Share learnings publicly ("Here's what I learned from beta testing...")
‚úÖ Thank people even if they say no
‚úÖ Follow up (gently) if they ghost after signing up

### DON'T:
‚ùå Spam every group at once
‚ùå Ignore community rules
‚ùå Oversell ("REVOLUTIONARY TOOL!!!")
‚ùå Get defensive about negative feedback
‚ùå Give up after first post gets no responses

### Timing:
- **Best days**: Tuesday-Thursday
- **Best times**: 9-11am, 7-9pm (local time for US/UK)
- **Avoid**: Monday mornings, Friday evenings, weekends

### Follow-Up Cadence:
- Day 0: Welcome email
- Day 3: "Any issues getting set up?"
- Day 7: "Has it worked for you?"
- Day 14: "What do you think?"
- Day 21: "Would you pay for this?"
- Day 28: Final survey + thank you

## Reality Check

**Expected conversion:**
- 100 people see your post
- 10-20 people comment/DM (10-20%)
- 5-10 people actually sign up (5-10%)
- 3-5 people actively use it (3-5%)
- 1-2 people would pay (1-2%)

**That's normal.** You need to reach 500-1000 people to get 10 active beta testers.

**Don't get discouraged.** Keep posting, keep following up, keep improving the product based on feedback.

One paying customer proves the concept. Five paying customers = you have a business.
